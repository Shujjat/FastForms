# Data Processing Agreement — template (draft)

**Not legal advice.** Have counsel adapt this for your entity and customers.

This agreement is between **[Customer]** (“Controller”) and **[Your company]** (“Processor”) regarding FastForms-hosted processing of personal data in form responses and related account data.

1. **Subject matter** Processing to provide the FastForms service as described in the subscription or order.
2. **Duration** For the term of the agreement and until data is returned or deleted per the Controller’s instructions or the service privacy policy.
3. **Nature and purpose** Storage, display to authorized users, export, and security/backup operations as configured.
4. **Types of data** As described in [PRIVACY.md](PRIVACY.md) (e.g. account data, form content, responses).
5. **Categories of data subjects** Form owners, respondents, and collaborators as applicable.
6. **Obligations of Processor** Process only on documented instructions; assist with data subject requests where feasible; implement appropriate technical and organizational measures; subprocessors per [SUBPROCESSORS.md](SUBPROCESSORS.md) with notice/authorization as required; delete or return data at end of service unless law requires retention.
7. **International transfers** As listed in the Privacy Policy (e.g. SCCs where applicable).
